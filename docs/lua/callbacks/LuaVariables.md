# LuaVariables

### > `mobileControls(CoolVars.mobileControls, ;)`

#### Whether mobile controls are active. @note Platform/runtime dependent. 

---

### > `Function_Stop(CoolVars.Function_Stop, ;)`

#### Special value to stop a Lua callback early. @note Return this from an event to cancel further handling. 

---

### > `Function_Continue(CoolVars.Function_Continue, ;)`

#### Special value to continue a Lua callback. @note Default flow; provided for parity with Function_Stop. 

---

### > `screenWidth(FlxG.width, ;)`

#### Game viewport width in pixels. 

---

### > `screenHeight(FlxG.height, ;)`

#### Game viewport height in pixels. 

---

### > `version(CoolVars.engineVersion, ;)`

#### Engine version string. 

---

### > `downscroll(ClientPrefs.data.downScroll, ;)`

#### Whether notes scroll downward instead of upward. 

---

### > `framerate(ClientPrefs.data.framerate, ;)`

#### Target frame rate from client preferences. 

---

### > `ghostTapping(ClientPrefs.data.ghostTapping, ;)`

#### Whether ghost tapping is enabled. 

---

### > `flashingLights(ClientPrefs.data.flashing, ;)`

#### Whether flashing light effects are enabled. 

---

### > `noteOffset(ClientPrefs.data.noteOffset, ;)`

#### Global note timing offset, in milliseconds. 

---

### > `noResetButton(ClientPrefs.data.noReset, ;)`

#### Whether the Reset keybind is disabled. 

---

### > `lowQuality(ClientPrefs.data.lowQuality, ;)`

#### Whether low-quality mode is enabled. 

---

### > `shadersEnabled(ClientPrefs.data.shaders, ;)`

#### Whether shaders are enabled. 

---

### > `scriptName(lua.name, ;)`

#### Name of the currently running Lua script. 

---

### > `currentModDirectory(Mods.folder, ;)`

#### Name of the active mod directory.
